A Further Look Into Video Game Sales
The bar chart on the left depicts ten of the best selling and most influential games and their sales figures in each of the major sections of the world. A lot of these sales are seen to be within the North American region, and the game "Wii Sports" can be seen as the highest selling game of all time, with sales numbers greater than 80 million copies sold. "Wii Sports" is wildly known as the most successful game and ended up skyrocketing the "Wii" console's sales also making it one of the most high selling video game consoles. This was because of "Wii Sport" having some incredibly innovative gameplay, utilising infrared sensors and motion controls. Nine out of ten of the games featured within this graph were produced by Nintendo, and can thank their family friendly themes and multiplayer gameplay for their high sales numbers.
The pie chart on the right depicts something rather interesting, it shows the sales numbers of different video game genres in different sections of the world, similar to the bar chart, it has the same regions defined. A very interesting fact displayed by this graph is the difference in the most popular genre in the North American region versus Japan. By selecting these sections in the drop down menu, it can be viewed that within North America, Action and Sports games are the most popular and highest selling, where as within Japan, Role Playing Games (RPGs) are the most popular. This could be explained by the differing cultures within Japan and North America, with North America being more interested in games such as "Call Of Duty" and "Fifa" rather than RPG games such as "Final Fantasy" and "Xenoblade Chronicles", which have been wildly successful within Japan. People in North America would also be purchasing video games for a younger audience, which would not have the attention span required for large fifty plus hour completion time RPG games.
The Most Successful Video Game Company
Referring back to the bar chart above, nine out of ten of the top selling games around the world are created by Nintendo. Nintendo, founded in 1989 by Fusajiro Yamauchi, is widely known as the most successful video game company bringing in billions of dollars in revenue and creating some of the most iconic and well known games and mascots in the world today. Looking at the line graph below, we can see the sheer amount of sales thic company has had over the years.
